## Overview

Zumba Wear's global store needed to match the energy of the Zumba fitness movement. We developed a Shopify Plus storefront with dynamic visuals, international shipping logic, and a community-driven shopping experience.

## Key Features

- Energy-driven theme design with bold typography and motion elements
- International shipping and multi-currency support for global customers
- Instructor-specific collections tied to the Zumba community program
- Size guide integration with fit recommendations based on activity type
