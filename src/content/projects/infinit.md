## Overview

Infinit's sports nutrition platform needed to combine scientific credibility with accessible shopping. We developed a Shopify store with detailed nutritional information, goal-based product discovery, and subscription capabilities.

## Key Features

- Goal-based product finder helping athletes select supplements by training objectives
- Detailed nutritional information panels with ingredient transparency
- Subscription and auto-replenishment system integrated with fulfillment
- Integration with logistics and payment providers across Latin America
