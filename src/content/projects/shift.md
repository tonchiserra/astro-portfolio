## Overview

Shift Shop's multi-category retail model required flexible Shopify architecture presenting diverse product lines without overwhelming shoppers. We designed an intuitive storefront with smart navigation and category-specific layouts.

## Key Features

- Category-adaptive layouts shifting design elements based on product vertical
- Smart navigation and mega-menu system for effortless browsing
- Personalized product recommendation engine based on browsing history
- Unified cart supporting mixed-category orders with intelligent shipping
