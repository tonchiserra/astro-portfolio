## Overview

Rusty Argentina needed a Shopify Plus store authentically representing their surf heritage with a modern, frictionless shopping experience. We developed a visually driven storefront with lifestyle content integration and seasonal collection showcases.

## Key Features

- Lifestyle-first design with full-screen hero sections and surf photography
- Seasonal collection launch pages with lookbook-style presentation
- Wetsuit-specific sizing charts and technical specification displays
- Argentine market optimizations including local payment methods and shipping
