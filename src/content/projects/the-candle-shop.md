## Overview

The Candle Shop needed an e-commerce platform that conveyed the warmth and craftsmanship behind their artisan candle collection. We built a Shopify store focused on visual storytelling and seamless purchasing.

## Key Features

- Clean, warm-toned theme design highlighting product photography and scent descriptions
- Custom scent finder quiz to guide customers to their ideal candle match
- Subscription model integration for recurring candle deliveries
- Gift builder feature allowing customers to create custom candle sets
