## Overview

MAC Cosmetics required a robust Shopify presence in the Dominican Republic maintaining globally recognized brand standards while adapting to local market needs. We delivered a high-performance storefront with shade-finder tools and localized payment options.

## Key Features

- Shade-matching tools integrated into the product experience to reduce return rates
- Localized payment gateway integration supporting Dominican banking infrastructure
- Seamless connection with MAC's global inventory and order management systems
- Mobile-first responsive design optimized for the region's mobile shopping audience
