Cole Haan's expansion into the Spanish market required a fully localized Shopify Plus storefront upholding their luxury brand identity. We developed a custom theme with region-specific payment methods, Euro pricing, and multilingual support.

- Custom Shopify Plus theme reflecting Cole Haan's premium brand aesthetic

- Multi-currency and multilingual support for the European audience
