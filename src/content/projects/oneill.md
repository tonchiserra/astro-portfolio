## Overview

O'Neill Argentina transitioned from a legacy e-commerce platform to Shopify for flexibility and performance. We handled the full migration including product data, customer accounts, and URL redirects while building a new storefront capturing the brand's surf heritage.

## Key Features

- Complete platform migration with zero-downtime cutover preserving SEO rankings
- Lifestyle-driven content sections blending surf photography with shoppable collections
- Advanced filtering by sport activity, season, and product category
- Integrated blog connecting brand storytelling with product discovery
