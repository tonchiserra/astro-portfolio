## Overview

L'Occitane Argentina needed a Shopify Plus store conveying the sensory richness of their Provencal beauty products while meeting Argentine e-commerce demands. We built a fully integrated storefront with local payment methods and a loyalty program.

## Key Features

- Immersive product detail pages with ingredient storytelling and rich media galleries
- Integration with Mercado Pago and installment-based payment plans
- ERP and inventory management system integration for real-time stock sync
- Custom loyalty and rewards program driving customer retention
