## Overview

Nestle Argentina's move to direct-to-consumer e-commerce required a Shopify Plus platform managing a vast product catalog spanning multiple food and beverage categories with complex integration requirements.

## Key Features

- Enterprise-scale product catalog supporting hundreds of SKUs across multiple brand lines
- Deep integration with ERP, warehouse management, and logistics systems
- Subscription and auto-replenishment functionality for consumable product lines
- Advanced promotional engine supporting bundle deals and volume discounts
