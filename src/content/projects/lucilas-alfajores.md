## Overview

Lucila's Alfajores needed an e-commerce platform conveying artisanal quality while enabling domestic and international gift orders. We developed a Shopify Plus store with gift-box customization and cold-chain logistics integrations.

## Key Features

- Gift-box builder for custom assortments with personalized messaging
- Subscription program with flexible frequency and flavor rotation options
- Integration with temperature-controlled logistics providers
- Artisanal brand storytelling with behind-the-scenes content
