## Overview

Havaianas Argentina needed a Shopify store embodying their iconic Brazilian lifestyle brand while catering to the local market. We developed a visually rich, fast-loading storefront with intuitive size selection and color-variant browsing.

## Key Features

- Color-swatch navigation for quick variant selection across their extensive palette
- Seasonal campaign modules with countdown timers and limited-edition launches
- Optimized performance for high-traffic summer sales periods
- Local payment method integration including installment plans
