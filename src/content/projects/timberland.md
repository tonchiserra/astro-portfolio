## Overview

Timberland's digital presence needed to reflect their heritage of quality outdoor footwear while meeting modern e-commerce expectations. We developed a Shopify storefront built for performance, scalability, and brand storytelling.

## Key Features

- High-performance theme with rich product detail pages showcasing material innovation
- Advanced filtering system for activity type, weather conditions, and terrain
- Store locator integration connecting online browsing to nearby retail locations
- Responsive design optimized for mobile-first outdoor enthusiasts
