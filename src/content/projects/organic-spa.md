## Overview

Organic Spa required an e-commerce platform that reflected their commitment to natural ingredients and sustainable beauty. We created a Shopify store with a clean, serene design that builds trust and drives conversions.

## Key Features

- Minimalist theme design with soft color palette reflecting natural brand values
- Ingredient transparency sections with detailed sourcing information per product
- Skin routine builder guiding customers through personalized product selections
- Eco-friendly shipping calculator showing carbon footprint per order
