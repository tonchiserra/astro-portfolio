## Overview

Fallen Footwear's Argentine store needed to speak directly to the local skateboarding community while showcasing technical durability. We built a Shopify Plus store with raw, authentic aesthetic and team-rider content integration.

## Key Features

- Skate culture-inspired design with raw typography and video backgrounds
- Technical product pages highlighting sole technology and durability features
- Team and community section connecting sponsored riders with collections
- Local payment and shipping integrations for the Argentine skate market
